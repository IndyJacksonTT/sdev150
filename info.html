Info

The Cursed Ark is a friendly and chill Ark community dedicated to providing a superior Ark experience for player's who don't have a ton of time but don't want to play on a super boosted server.

We do this with higher yet still close to vanilla rates. "Quality of Life" mods to reduce the tedious bits of the game that suck your life away; and fun events to keep you entertained!

We offer a superior experience to vanilla Ark by having tons of modded dinos that have been balanced to not overshadow vanilla tames and blend in perfectly!

Our hybrid PVP/PVE system lets players play how they want. If you want to wage war and destroy your enemies or build beautiful kingdoms the Cursed Ark is the place for you!
